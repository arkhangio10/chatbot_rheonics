# Bluetooth Low-Energy (BLE) connection

## Pairing

Pairing the SMET to another Bluetooth device can be performed in two ways:

  * If a display is attached to the SMET, the pairing passcode will be shown on the display, and must be entered (or simply confirmed) on the other device.
  * If no display is attached, the passcode is shown on the other device and must be entered into SMET using the `xwbtkey` RCP command, e.g. `xwbtkey 123456`.
